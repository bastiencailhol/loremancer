About Loremancer

The project

Loremancer is a character creation tool designed for concept artists and illustrators in the fantasy genre.
Whether you're stuck in a design phase or seeking fresh ideas, Loremancer helps make the magic happen!

Inspiration: Loremancer taps into the power of RNG to provide countless unique combinations.

Customization: Once satisfied with the suggestions, lock the elements and fine-tune the details to bring your vision to life.

Visualization: Explore a curated gallery of over 10,000 handpicked references, each linked to your chosen attributes.

The creator

Loremancer is developed and maintained by Bastien Cailhol, a developer passionate about design and fantasy. This project was born from his desire to help artists push the boundaries of their creativity by gamifying the process.
