About Loremancer

The project

Loremancer is your creative sidekick in the land of fantasy. Whether you're stuck in a design phase or seeking fresh ideas, Loremancer helps make the magic happen!

Inspiration: RNG-powered, because nothing says creativity like a well-placed dice roll.

Customization: Once satisfied with the suggestions, lock the elements and fine-tune your characters to the point where even their backstory has a backstory.

Visualization: Finding strong references shouldn't feel like a side quest—it should be the main story arc.

The creator

Loremancer is the brainchild of Bastien Cailhol, a developer with a passion for fantasy worlds and clever design. He gamified the character designing process to help fellow artists level up their imagination.

Special thanks to Sylvain Marty for his invaluable help!
